I stumbled into a couple Olivia signals
on 14071kHz,
and on a good guess,
I fired up `fldigi`,
and I was able to decode them.
They were Olivia-8/250 encoded.
I found lots of
[good documents on Olivia](https://groups.io/g/Olivia)
to read some mmore about where they're likely found.
